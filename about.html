Who we are
01

A design-first
packaging studio

Harveston was built on a single conviction: that great packaging is a design problem before it is a manufacturing problem. We assemble briefs, question assumptions, sketch structures and refine finishes before a single board is cut.

Our team combines structural engineers, brand designers and production specialists — working together from the first conversation to the final delivery. This integration is what allows us to move fast, iterate without waste, and produce work that stands out.

We work with brands at every stage — from founders launching their first product to established names rethinking a legacy range. The common thread is ambition: clients who want packaging that does more than contain.

"We don't produce packaging.
We design it — then produce it."
Our design capability

Where our strength
actually lies

Most packaging suppliers are factories with a catalogue. We are designers with a factory. That difference shapes everything — how we interpret your brief, how we solve structural problems, and how we make your brand look better than it did before.

Brand-led design
We read your brand before we pick a material. Every structural and finish decision serves the brand identity — not the other way around.
Structural engineering
Our structural team designs from first principles. Dielines, wall geometry and insert systems are engineered in-house — not adapted from standard templates.
Finish expertise
Foil, emboss, soft touch, spot UV, deboss — we know which finish earns its cost and which is decorative noise. We advise, not upsell.
100% design refund
If you are not satisfied with the first round of concepts, we refund the design fee in full. No conditions. It keeps us focused on getting it right.
