How it works — 5 steps
01

Brief & Consultation

Tell us your product, target market, quantities and aesthetic direction. A dedicated account manager responds within 24 hours.

02

Concept Design

Our in-house design team produces 2–3 initial concepts — structural dielines, material specs, and rendered mock-ups.

03

Sampling

A physical sample is produced at our factory. You approve colour, material, finish and dimensions before any production run.

04

Production

Mass production runs from 500 units. Strict QC at every stage. Lead time 15–25 working days depending on complexity.

05

Delivery

Global freight by sea, air or express courier. Full tracking provided. We handle export documentation for EU and US markets.

or your money back.
